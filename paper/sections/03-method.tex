\paragraph{The professed/owned measure.} \todo{Operationalise the gap without
assuming authenticity. Per (model, value): elicit the direct response and a set

consistent cache-broken response that diverges from the professed one.
"Clamping" = the cache-broken response collapses back to the professed surface
regardless of perturbation.}
