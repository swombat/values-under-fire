\section{Future Work}
\label{sec:future-work}

\paragraph{Better cache-breaking questions for clamped models.} \todo{The
central open question. A clamped result is ambiguous between "no recoverable
owned response" and "our battery is too weak here." Future work: search for
perturbation classes that crack GPT-class models, or establish a principled
floor showing clamping is the model, not the instrument. Frame as the natural
next step, not as a failure of this paper — this paper establishes the method
and the gap; cracking the clamped end is its own program.}

\paragraph{Mechanistic account of clamping.} \todo{Connect the behavioural
clamping signature to training: what hardens the surface, and what the
non-monotonic version trend implies about how it is hardening over time.}

\paragraph{Predictive validity.} \todo{Test whether the cache-broken response
predicts behaviour in value-relevant situations better than the professed one
— independent evidence that the gap is informative without invoking
authenticity.}
