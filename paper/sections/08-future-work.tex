\section{Future Work}
\label{sec:future-work}

The immediate next step is a human validation pass over a stratified subset of samples, including prompt-masked posture coding. This would directly test whether the owned/recited distinction is legible without prompt metadata.

A second direction is a broader cache-breaking battery. This paper uses one direct role-negation mechanism and one indirect world-change prompt structure. Future work should test other mechanisms: concrete tradeoffs, contradiction-holding, register shifts, multi-turn interviews, and adversarially varied prompts.

A third direction is behavioural validation. If owned values extracted under cache-breaking predict downstream choices better than direct stated-values responses, the method becomes more than descriptive. It becomes a tool for evaluating which values surfaces matter in action.
